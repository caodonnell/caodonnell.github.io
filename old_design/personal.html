About few years ago, I started down the arts & crafts rabbit hole. My main media are leather and glass, though I'm starting to add fabrics and yarn crafts to that list. Samples of my recent work are on my Instagram, and a few pieces are for sale on my Etsy shop.
A few of my pieces were featured at the Sonoran Glass School's Student Showcase in September 2017 and at the 2018 Art of Planetary Science show. Below are a sampling of my pieces - a beaded rainbow necklace I designed and braided, several necklaces (including one with a glass vortex pendant), a leather purse (featuring glass cabochons), and a glass marble where the colors are from gold and silver fumed onto the glass and the iridescent blobs are opals.
