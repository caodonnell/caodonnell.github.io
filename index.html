Christine O'Donnell, Ph.D.

I am passionate about investigating how the Universe works and sharing that knowledge with others. I am currently an Education & Diversity Program Manager at the American Physical Society (APS), the largest professional society for physicists in the US. My previous education research experiences include creating and implementing culturally responsive science curriculum and associated professional development in order to empower students to be able to engage with and critique science (including both content and cultural norms) to improve society.
